Farrow with Blacks Bean and Fresh Herb

Description

Farro is an ancient whole grain that is back in style. When cooked, it’s slightly chewy and a bit creamy with a mild, nutty flavor. Toss it with beans, fresh herbs and veggies for a quick and satisfying entrée.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup farro (see note)
  • 1 small red pepper, finely diced
  • 1 small yellow or orange pepper, finely diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 1 small lemon, juiced
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked or 1 (15 ounce) can low sodium black beans, drained
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin, optional

Steps

  1. Cook farro according to package instructions.
  2. Place in a large bowl and mix in remaining ingredients.

Note: Look for whole grain or semi-pearled farro. Pearled farro cooks faster but the nutritious germ and bran have been removed.
